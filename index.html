Laser Hair Removal

Laser hair removal uses intense, pulsating beams of light to remove unwanted hair from the face, underarms, back, legs and bikini area. Learn more here.

Laser Tattoo Removal

Laser treatment for tattoo removal has improved significantly over the years, making it easier and less painful to remove unwanted tattoos extra extra extra extra.

Photodynamic Therapy

UCSF surgeons specialize in an advanced treatment called photodynamic therapy, or PDT. PDT is used to treat rough, scaly and dry growths on the face and scalp.

Sclerotherapy

Soft Tissue Fillers

As part of the aging process, the collagen, hyaluronic acid and elasticity in skin decreases, causing wrinkles and a loss of fullness and volume in the face.
